ReactJS Lifecycle:
	1. Initialization
		constructor(props)
		render()

	2. Mounting
		componentDidMount()
		render()

	3. Updation <- setState() or due to change of props 
		render()
		componentDidUpdate()

	4. Unmounting
		componentWillUnmount()
